Divergence Arrows
D — Green arrow (Bullish Divergence)
Price made a new LOW but MACD histogram did NOT — sell momentum is weakening even though price is still dropping. Often precedes a bounce. Look for this at support levels.
Below candle  |  Price falling, MACD turning up = potential BUY
D — Red arrow (Bearish Divergence)
Price made a new HIGH but MACD histogram did NOT — buying momentum is fading even though price keeps rising. Warning the move is running out of steam. Watch for reversal.
Above candle  |  Price rising, MACD weakening = potential SELL
hD — Teal arrow (Hidden Divergence)
Price made a HIGHER low but MACD made a lower low. Happens during pullbacks inside an uptrend — confirms the trend is still intact and the dip is a buying opportunity. Best for trend continuation entries.
Below candle  |  Buy the dip in an uptrend
Supertrend Flip Circles
● Cyan circle — Supertrend flipped UP
The Supertrend indicator just changed direction from bearish to bullish on this exact candle. The green line switched from above to below price. One of the strongest single reversal signals — acts fast and early.
Below candle  |  Often the start of a new uptrend
● Orange circle — Supertrend flipped DOWN
The Supertrend changed from bullish to bearish on this candle. The support line crossed above price — warning that the uptrend may be ending. Prepare for possible short or put.
Above candle  |  Often the start of a new downtrend

Market Direction of the Day
How it is decided:
The badge evaluates a 5-Factor Confluence model in real-time, monitoring:
  • SPY 1m & 5m Trends (Supertrend): Price direction on quick and macro timeframes.
  • QQQ 1m Trend: Tech correlation check (ensures QQQ is moving with SPY).
  • MACD BB (1m): Confirms expanding buying/selling momentum.
  • NYSE ADD (Market Breadth): Measures the number of advancing vs falling stocks. (Acts as a filter to reject false, low-volume breakouts).
How to trade it:
🐂 STRONG BULLISH Score ≥ 4/5. High-probability uptrend. Bias: Long / CALL setups only. Never short.
📈 MILD BULLISH Score = 3/5. Moderate upward bias. Be selective, lock in profits quickly.
⚖️ NEUTRAL / SIDEWAYS Mixed indicators. Expect choppy channels. Reduce position sizes or stand aside.
📉 MILD BEARISH Score = 2/5. Moderate downward bias. Watch for reversal triggers.
🐻 STRONG BEARISH Score ≤ 1/5. Heavy selling pressure. Bias: Short / PUT setups only. Never go long.
